Kobe Bryant

Here`s the history of Kobe bryant A.K.A "Black mamba"

  • 1978 - Was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1996 - He was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets in the 13th position of the draft,
    he was traded the same day to Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 2000 - 2002 - Lakers won 3 consecutive NBA Finals, being Kobe Bryant important
    in each one of them.
  • 2006 - January of this year Kobe Bryant scored 81 points in one game of regular season,
    turning him in the 2nd player with more points socred in one game.
  • 2008 - In this season Kobe Bryant won his first an only MVP of the regular season.
  • 2009 - 2010 - Two consecutive years winning again the NBA Finals, but this time he won the
    MVP Finals making him super important in both Finals.
  • 2016 - This was the last season of Kobe, the last game he played was against Utah Jazz and he scored 60 points.
  • 2020 - January 26th, 41 years old Kobe Bryant and his 13th years old daughter Gianna suffered an helicopter accident,
    later that day the authorities confirmed that Kobe Bryant and the other 9 passengers died that day.
